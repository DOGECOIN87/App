CMYK Video Channel Extractor

Supported Video Formats: MP4, WebM, Ogg

Instructions: Upload a video, select a CMYK channel, and click "Process Video" to extract that channel as grayscale.

Browser Compatibility Note: Format compatibility will be detected automatically.

Troubleshooting: If video loading fails, try the "Enable Processing" button or convert to a different format.
